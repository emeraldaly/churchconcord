Online Service

The player will only display 10 minutes before and after the Sunday service and Wednesday meeting. You will either see a photo of the church (and then you can push play and listen) or code as a placeholder for the temporarily deactivatived player.

Ten minutes before the service, we encourage you to refresh this page to make sure the player loads. Contact the Clerk with any questions.

During the testimony portion of the Wednesday meeting, call Rose at 603 848 8530 to give a testimony. Alternately, you can email clerk@christianscienceconcordnh.org to submit a testimony in advance to be read during the service. Be aware that there is a lag in sound, so once you're acknowledged it is recommended that you reduce your computer's sound while you speak and then increase it to continue listening.
