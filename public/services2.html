Sunday Service

Our Sunday service is held at 10:30 am in the edifice. Our Pastor is the Bible and our textbook Science and Health with Key to the Scriptures, given to us by the founder of Christian Science, Mary Baker Eddy. Each Sunday we hear readings from this timeless pastor in a Bible Lesson which is published in our Christian Science Quarterly, available for study all week long and read on Sunday by readers elected to serve in that capacity at services all around the world!

Wednesday Service

Every Wednesday evening at 7:00 pm, we gather in the edifice to celebrate the healings and inspiration that come from the study and application of Chiristian Science. After readings from the Bible and Science and Health, the meeting is open to the attendees to share testimonies of healing. This midweek meeting has brought healings to those who attend and serves as a very welcome mid-week spiritual lift.

Sunday School

We offer Sunday School at the same time as our Sunday service at 10:30 am in the Sunday School Building next door. Children are taught the Bible stories that lovingly show their inseparable kinship to God and his Christ. Young adults up to the age of 20 are guided in their unfolding understanding of God. All are taught by dedicated teachers who have had healings in Christian Science and love sharing and learning with their students.

Childcare

Infant and child care is provided for children under the age of 3 on Sundays and for children not old enough to attend the Wednesday evening testimony meeting. The nursery is located in the Sunday School building.

Parking

Parking is located in a lot behind the Sunday School and on the street. Street parking is free on Sundays and after 7pm during the week.
