Christian Science Resources

This church is a branch of the Mother Church in Boston, MA. To learn more about Christian Science, there are several official websites that have helpful information. We encourage you to spend time on these sites, to visit a reading room, or ask an usher at a service if you have any questions.

Official Christian Science Site

Learn more about Christian Science, Mary Baker Eddy, our global services, and access contact information for Christian Science practitioners and nurses among other information.

Visit

JSH-Online

This is an online portal to access inspirational articles in The Christian Science Journal, Sentinel, and Herald. A subscription is needed to access much of the content-- visit our reading room to browse for free!

Visit

Mary Baker Eddy Library

Find information about visiting the Library where you can explore the life of Mary Baker Eddy. A popular attraction is the Mapparium!

Visit

Christian Science Monitor

The Christian Science Monitor is an internationally acclaimed news source for over 108 years-- delivering high-quality, distinctive journalism. Be sure to check out both the Daily digital access and weekly print and digital magazine. Both are available at our Reading Room.

Visit

Longyear Museum

Longyear Museum is an independent historial museum dedicated to advancing the understanding of the life and work of Mary Baker Eddy. In addition to the museum in Boston, they maintain and offer tours of several of Mary Baker Eddy's historical homes. Be sure to check out the home on North State Street in Concord.

Visit

Christian Science Lectures

Learn more about Christian Science by listening to a public lecture. Some are shared on the official CS Lecture page. Stay tuned to hear about local lectures coming to your area.
