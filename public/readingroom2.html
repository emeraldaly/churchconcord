Reading Room

You are welcome to visit our Reading Room! It is located on North Main Street at 34 Main Street. You will find a pleasant atmosphere and a friendly staff to welcome you.

The Reading Room is a great resource where the Bible, biblical reference works, all the writings of Mary Baker Eddy, and our timely and lively periodicals including the award-winning Christian Science Monitor are available to read, borrow or purchase.

The Reading Room has a treasure of articles, records of healings and inspiring materials, to help you overcome any and all of your challenges.
