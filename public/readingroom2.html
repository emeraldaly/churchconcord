Reading Room

   

You are welcome to visit our Reading Room in the heart of downtown Concord at 34 North Main Street. A welcoming staff will be on hand to greet you, to help answer questions, or to guide you in finding resources to borrow, to use, or to purchase.

In addition to the sales room, the Reading Room offers a quiet study room where you may stay for a few minutes or a few hours. In the study room you will find the Bible, all of the writings of Mary Baker Eddy, reference tools, and current copies of periodicals that inspire and offer hope to today's readers. Also available is The Christian Science Monitor, an award-winning news magazine, that grapples with and offers perspectives and solutions on a wide-range of topics.

A treasure trove of more than a hundred years of articles and documented healings of all kinds may be found in our bound volumes. Come in and explore some of the "staff picks" or explore these inspiring resources yourself and find your own favorite testimonies and articles. Blessings await you in the Reading Room!
