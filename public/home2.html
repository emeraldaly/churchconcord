What We Do

Mary Baker Eddy, a native of NH, discovered and founded Christian Science, defined as "the law of God, the law of good..." (Rudimental Divine Science, p.1). The foundation for the theology and practice of Christian Science is rooted firmly in the Bible.

The Christian Science Concord Church is a branch of The Mother Church, the First Church of Christ, Scientist, Boston, MA. The Mother Church and its thousands of branches world-wide have the collective mission to "re-instate primitive Christianty and its lost element of healing."

We are a community dedicated to understanding the Bible and applying its truths to our lives in ways that gets results. We hope to see you at a future service or in our Reading Room.

Our edifice has recently undergone quite a bit of preservation work. We are grateful for the many donors who made this work possible and look forward to sharing the results with the public and congregation.
