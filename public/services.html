Sunday Service - 10:30 am
The Bible and our textbook, Science and Health with Key to the Scriptures, by the founder of Christian Science, Mary Baker Eddy, are our only pastor.

Each Sunday, we hear readings from a Bible lesson published in the Christian Science Quarterly. The Bible Lesson is available for study during the week and is read by lay readers elected by the membership.

Wednesday Service - 7:00 pm
Every Wednesday evening at 7:00 pm, we hold a testimony meeting. After topical readings selected from the Bible and Science and Health, the meeting is open to attendees to share testimonies of healing and inspiration gained through the study of Christian Science.
Sunday School - 10:30 am
Sunday school is held at the same time as our Sunday service in the Sunday School building next door. Students learn how the teachings of the Bible can help them in their daily lives. Children up to the age of 20 are welcome.


Childcare
Childcare for infants and toddlers is lovingly provided in the Sunday School Building at all of our services.
Parking
Parking is located in a lot behind the Sunday School and on the street. Street parking is free on Sundays and after 7pm during the week.
